Why would people go for Dove go soft?

Group work project

Go soft is a brand extension for a fabric softener by Dove brand. I was the researcher in a group of 7 people. After the research and lots of surveys with different gender and age group, I summarised the most important information that would make the consumer go for Dove go soft in my final piece of infographics.
