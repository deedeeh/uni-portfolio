Forever Together Demo

Forever Together is a phone application for brides especially the ones who are working and busy. All the bride has to do is to scan herself, try on various dresses, shoes, accessories and veils through the phone screen then choose what she favourite and book an appointment to try things one last time. The idea is to save time for brides and give them a general idea about what they really love to wear in their special day. Also, brides could choose from cakes selection and wedding invitations.
