Sweet Revenge!

Sweet revenge is a handmade action drama comic book. The story is about a character called Hitchair lost his wife and daughter who were killed by the villain Ninjo. The book showed the sad memories that Hitchair has been through and in the end he got his revenge from Ninjo by the help of his friends.
